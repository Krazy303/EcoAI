EcoAI Pro Tips

1

Rinse Before You Bin

One dirty yogurt container can contaminate an entire bale of recycling. A quick 3-second rinse makes a world of difference.

2

The "Cap On" Rule

Modern facilities prefer caps screwed tightly onto empty bottles. Loose caps often fall through machinery and become landfill.

3

Benefits of Composting

Composting reduces methane emissions from landfills and creates "black gold" — nutrient-rich soil that sequester carbon.

4

Say No to Plastic Bags

Never put your recycling in a plastic bag. Workers are instructed to throw the whole bag away to keep the machines from jamming.
